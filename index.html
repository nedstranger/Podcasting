Why podcasts work

For high-value B2B

In high-value B2B, the bottleneck is usually not traffic. It is access to the right decision-makers, and trust once you have their attention. A podcast helps with both.

The logic

  1. You want conversations with a small number of hard-to-reach decision-makers.
  2. Cold outreach is easy to ignore.
  3. A podcast gives you a credible reason to ask for time.
  4. You get 30–60 minutes of focused conversation, on record.
  5. The episode becomes proof of your expertise, standards, and network.
  6. Guests often share it because it makes them sound strong.
  7. You stay top-of-mind when a contract or referral opportunity appears.

What you’re really buying

  • Access to people who may ignore a sales email
  • Trust built through a proper conversation
  • Credibility you can reuse with future prospects
  • Visibility inside your guests’ networks
Access → Trust → Referrals → Revenue
